AuraDrop Privacy Policy

AuraDrop is a Tampermonkey script that adds playful context labels to publicly visible LinkedIn feed posts.

Data Collection

  • AuraDrop only reads text from publicly visible posts in your LinkedIn feed.
  • No private messages, profile data, or personal identifiers are collected.
  • No personally identifiable information (PII), health, financial, authentication, location, or browsing data is collected.

Data Usage

  • Text is sent to a remote service to generate a context label and brief explanation.
  • The remote service does not store or reuse your post text.
  • Labels are cached locally in your browser for efficiency; the cache stores only a hash and the label, not the original post.

Permissions

  • Storage: used for local caching only.
  • Host permissions: used to read publicly visible LinkedIn posts and communicate with the backend service.

No Advertising, Tracking, or Sharing

AuraDrop does not track your activity, show ads, or share data with third parties.
